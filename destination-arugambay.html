Arugam Bay

The Legendary Surf Haven of the East

About Arugam Bay

Welcome to Arugam Bay, a legendary name in the global surf community and the undisputed surf capital of Sri Lanka's east coast. With its world-class waves, golden beaches, and an irresistibly laid-back atmosphere, 'A-Bay' is a must-visit destination for surfers, adventurers, and anyone looking to unwind. From April to October, when the rest of the island's south-west coast is in its monsoon season, Arugam Bay comes alive with perfect swells and a vibrant, international crowd.

But there's more to Arugam Bay than just surfing. The area is a gateway to incredible wildlife and pristine nature. Nearby lagoons offer tranquil canoe safaris where you can spot crocodiles and exotic birds, while the adjacent Kumana and Lahugala National Parks are home to elephants, leopards, and a rich an array of fauna. Blending thrilling surf sessions with wild nature and ancient temples, Arugam Bay offers a unique and unforgettable Sri Lankan experience.

Key Attractions & Experiences

  • Surf 'The Point': Ride the famous long, right-hand point break that makes Arugam Bay a world-renowned surf destination.
  • Explore Other Surf Spots: Take a tuk-tuk to nearby breaks like Peanut Farm, Pottuvil Point, or Elephant Rock, suitable for various skill levels.
  • Go on a Wildlife Safari: Discover the leopards and abundant birdlife of Kumana National Park (Yala East) or see elephant herds at Lahugala.
  • Pottuvil Lagoon Safari: Enjoy a peaceful canoe or boat trip through the scenic lagoon to spot crocodiles, eagles, and other wildlife.
  • Discover Ancient Temples: Visit the historic Muhudu Maha Viharaya, an ancient Buddhist temple with seaside ruins.
  • Relax and Unwind: Embrace the chilled-out, bohemian vibe of the town, enjoy yoga classes, and relax in a beachfront cafe.

Quick Info

  • Best time: April - October
  • Location: Eastern Province
  • Category: Surfing, Beach, Wildlife
